Marian Croak

Biography

Marian Croak was born on May 14, 1995 in New York City. She attended the University of California and the University of Princeton where she obtained her Ph.D in Social Psychology and Qualitative Analysis. She worked in AT&T Bell Laboratories from 1982-2014 where she worked with voice and data communication and ranked Senior Vice President of Applications & Service Infrastructure. After leaving AT&T, she began working in Google as Vice President for research and development for access strategy and emerging markets. As of 2013, she is in the Women of Technology International Hall of Fame.
